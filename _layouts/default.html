What is PFactor?

PFactor.io is a free website that focuses on aviation, and electronics. Our aim is to provide you with interesting tutorials, articles, and resources that deal with these topics.

Why this name?

In electrical engineering, the phrase "power factor" (or PFactor) is one with special connotations. It represents the ratio of real power to total power generated by an electrical system.

In aviation, p-factor refers to the left-turning tendency of the aircraft caused by asymmetric loading of the propeller at high angles of attack.
